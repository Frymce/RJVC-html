Trois Communautés Unies

Pour mieux servir notre communauté grandissante, RJVC s'est structuré en trois pôles distincts mais interconnectés : l'Académie, les Événements, et Le Mouvement Annuel.

Le Mouvement Annuel

Our cornerstone annual event, a powerful multi-day experience that brings together the entire RJVC family for worship, teaching, and celebration.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to unite young Christians, empowering them to grow in their faith, serve their communities, and live lives that reflect the joy and love of Christ. We envision a generation on fire for God, transforming the world through unity and service.
